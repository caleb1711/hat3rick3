our training drills target your

ball control

Good control of the football is an essential skill to maintain possession of the ball from the opposition. To play effectively you must learn to control the ball. The most important touch you will make when controlling the ball is the first touch. This is when you get the ball and what you do with it when you first touch the ball.

techniques

Technique are vital for sports participation safety. High level technique help prevent injury and enhance the well-being of the athlete.

Dribbling

Dribbling is an important skill to develop. it is one of the most useful attacking moves . Dribbling helps you beat or eliminate defenders by running past them with the ball, to create new passing lanes and angles, and to maintain possession and control of the ball.

Passing & shooting

Ultimately, mastering passing allows players to connect and combine with each other successfully. This helps them control the ball and unlock stubborn defences with fast-flowing moves and combinations. Shooting is an important skill to develop, regardless of your position. It is the ultimate goal of all offensive tactics in football matches. This is the most basic way to score a goal and the only way to score a goal.
